Welcome to DeeOps

Hello! I’m Dee Sanas, a Solution Architect and Project Manager with over a decade of experience delivering high-performance, secure, and scalable cloud solutions for enterprises.

At Viettel Group, I’ve led the development of proprietary cloud platforms and hybrid solutions serving customers across Asia and Africa. My work includes architecting Viettel’s OpenStack-based private cloud, defining roadmaps for managed services, and improving deployment efficiency while maintaining high availability.

I created DeeOps to share my approach to cloud architecture, product management, and digital transformation. My goal is to help teams deliver modern cloud solutions that support business growth and national digital initiatives.
