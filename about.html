About Me

Hi, I'm Dee—a Solution Architect and Project Manager with over a decade of experience helping organizations design, deploy, and manage reliable, scalable technology solutions across cloud platforms, enterprise networking, automation frameworks, and large-scale systems.

My work has spanned public, private, and hybrid cloud environments, including AWS, Azure, and Viettel Cloud. I focus on building architectures that deliver real business value, leading teams to execute with clarity and precision, and ensuring solutions that scale.

I believe technology should solve problems, not create them. That’s why I prioritize clear strategies, collaborative planning, and hands-on execution to ensure every project delivers measurable value—whether it's modernizing infrastructure, improving security, or enabling growth.

What I Do

  • Define cloud and technology strategies aligned with organizational goals.
  • Design and deploy secure, high-availability infrastructures on AWS, Azure, Viettel Cloud, and hybrid platforms.
  • Lead projects with clear milestones, measurable outcomes, and effective stakeholder engagement.
  • Optimize operational costs and performance through automation, Infrastructure as Code (IaC), and continuous improvement.
  • Build resilient networks, automation pipelines, and disaster recovery solutions for mission-critical workloads.
  • Drive product strategy and roadmap development for cloud services.

I enjoy mentoring engineers, fostering strong partnerships, and continuously learning to stay ahead in a fast-evolving technology landscape. My goal is to make technology simpler, more reliable, and more impactful for organizations and their customers.
